Get CCTV Installation Leads At $6.11 Each.

Client: CCTV Installation Company (Melbourne Based Business)

Main Objective: Generate high-quality leads for CCTV installation at a lower cost than Google Ads (Current Average CPC $5.5).

The Challenge

The client was struggling to generate leads profitably. They were spending thousands on Google Ads, yet each lead was costing them a whopping $70; eating into their margins.

  1. Google Ads CPC for CCTV installation ranged from A$7.51 - A$9.15! Meaning lead costs quickly spiraled out of control.
  2. High competition was driving costs up, making it unsustainable for long-term growth.

They needed a smarter, more cost-effective way to get leads and grow their business profitably.

See for yourself - Here’s the CPC data from Google Keyword Planner: 

The Solution

Instead of competing in an overcrowded market on Google Ads, we redirected their budget to Facebook Ads. A platform where competition for CCTV services was significantly lower.

Why this worked:

  1. Hyper-Targeted Audience - Unlike Google Ads, where you pay per click, Facebook allowed us to precisely target homeowners & businesses looking for security solutions.
  2. Engaging Visuals & Messaging - Video ads & compelling creatives showcased the benefits of CCTV, increasing engagement.
  3. Geo-Targeted Ads - We focused on high-conversion locations, ensuring every dollar spent went toward real potential customers. 

New Facebook Ads Strategy Included:

✔ Optimised ad copy focused on security concerns & peace of mind.
✔ High-quality video & image creatives showcasing CCTV benefits.
✔ Geo-targeted ads for high-demand local areas.
✔ Lookalike audience targeting to find new high-intent customers.
✔ A/B testing to refine ad performance & maximise ROI. 

The Results

The Results: From $70 per Lead to Just $6.11!
